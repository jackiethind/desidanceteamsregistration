Meet the Instructors

Master Class Instructors

Brittnie Aguilar

Brittnie Aguilar was born and raised in Hawaii and started dancing at Hypersquad Dance Co at the age of 7. She was a part of Academy of Hype, achieving 1st place at World of Dance Hawaii 2010 and World of Dance LA 2011. Additionally, she was also part of Hype 5-0 who got 3rd place on America's Best Dance Crew Season 5. A crew she founded back in Hawaii is Blank Canvas Crew, opening The Playground Dance Studio. At the end of 2014, Brittnie moved to California to pursue her dream team, GRV. They have achieved 2nd place at Vibe 2015, 3rd place at Bridge 2016, and cool points at Body Rock. Brittnie is currently a choreographer for GRV and continues her dance career through traveling and teaching.

Gopi Engineer

Gopi Engineer is a dance artist, choreographer, instructor and performer whose dance journey spans over 30​ years in Bollywood, Indian folk and Indian classical dance along with contemporary and fusion styles. In the folk genre, she has engaged in garba, raas, launi and bhangra dance styles and in the classical dance she has trained in both Kathak and Bharatanatyam. As the founder and Artistic director of Meher Dance Company, Gopi teaches Bollywood and Indian dance to kids and adults in the Chicago land area, leads a professional dance troupe, teaches private choreography in person and online as well as engages in dance production​ projects internationally. She was the dance coordinator on the Bollywood film, Dhoom 3 and was the director of choreography for the Bengali film, Simanaheen. This past year, her company ​was featured in Buzzfeed Video's film "The Art of Dance" showcasing movement in 360 videography, the first of its kind. She is truly passionate about dance and advancing this art form, but nothing brings her more joy than to teach people how to dance and to encourage great talent like we see in DDN!

Varun Gurunath

Varun Gurunath is a veteran in the Bollywood dance industry. Having performed and taught for over 10 years, he is an acclaimed choreographer whose style blends Bollywood with the aesthetics of modern Hip Hop and the energy and masala of the South Indian film industry. He is currently one of the main instructors and choreographers at NDM Bollywood Dance Studios, the first Bollywood dance school in the United States. Varun's industry work includes assistant choreographer for Nakul Dev Mahajan on So You Think You Can Dance, Nickelodeon, NBC's The Office, America's Best Dance Crew, Prabhu Deva, and Natalie Portman. He strives to inspire and instill his dance expertise and knowledge to kids all over so they can love and pursue their passion.
